How to use

  • Start: Begins the stopwatch timer
  • Pause: Temporarily stops the timer
  • Reset: Resets the timer to 00:00:00 and clears all laps
  • Lap: Records the current time while the stopwatch continues running
  • Laps are displayed with the time they were recorded and the duration since the previous lap
